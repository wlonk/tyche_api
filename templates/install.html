Installing

Tyche is not available for public use yet.

If you have access to Tyche, you should have the OAuth link to invite Tyche to your server. Then, log in here and go to the "Servers" tab. Click on the server you want to configure, and you can set the following:

Prefix: what you should prefix commands with to make Tyche listen to them.

Cosmetic roles: the list of roles you have configured on your Discord server that grant no permissions, but that users can give or take from themselves. These might be pronouns, Starfleet ranks, or who knows what else!

Streaming role: the role, already configured on your Discord server, to give to users while they are streaming on Twitch. This should ideally be a role with no privileges, which is displayed separately from other roles, and ranked high enough to show up at or near the top.

Streaming role requires: the role, if any, to require before granting a user the streaming role. In the common case where you have many streamers on a server, but only some of them should get their streams advertised or promoted, this is the mechanism to acheive that.
